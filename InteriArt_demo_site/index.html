about us

Interiart is an award-winning architecture and interior design practice based in NYC. We work internationally on projects of residential & commercial interior design that require a creative approach. Our talented and experienced designers leverage their knowledge and expertise to create unique and comfortable interiors for you.

Our team knows that interior design can be stressful for the client and we do our best to make it as easy as possible. We listen to your needs, ideas, and inputs. And most importantly, we make it exciting and enjoyable for our clients.

COMMERCIAL DESIGN

We take a dedicated approach to help our commercial clients create the interior for their company offices and other spaces.

RESIDENTIAL DESIGN

Our residential interior design services are perfect for creating a cozy atmosphere in your house regardless of its size.

OUR SERVICES

Our diverse range of services has brought together a multidisciplinary team of professionals. Beyond simply providing interior design solutions, we take care of our clients’ safety and comfort.

Every service we offer our clients is a combination of professionalism, talent, innovations, and experience that our team incorporates into each of the projects we work on. Here are some of the services we offer.

INTERIOR DESIGN

Interior design services offer a vast variety of solutions for our clients’ homes and offices.

DECORATIVE SERVICES

SPACE PLANNING

PROJECT MANAGEMENT
